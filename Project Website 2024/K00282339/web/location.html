Our Location

Welcome to Zombies Incorporated, the COD: Black Ops 3 Zombies Club! Our gaming sessions take place at:

67 Gaming Avenue
Zombieville, Richtofen 772
Ireland

We're conveniently located near public transportation routes and offer ample parking for our members.

Directions:

From Downtown Zombieville:

Take the Zombiewalk Bus Line to Gaming Street.

Exit the bus and walk two kilometres east to reach our club.

Hours of Operation:

We're open for gaming sessions:

Monday to Friday: 6:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Saturday and Sunday: 12:00 PM - 8:00 PM
