So what is COD Zombies?

COD Zombies is a game mode is the extremely popular game franchise ‘Call of Duty’ published by Activision. The first game to have this mode was ‘World at War’ back in 2008 where the developers saw it as a nice, simple little extra mode unlocked after completely the campaign. They were quickly proved wrong once players discovered this mode and it rapidly grew to popularity. Realizing the love for the mode, the developers continued to add Zombies to many of the future COD titles. These titles include Call of Duty: World at War, Call of Duty: Black Ops, Call of Duty: Black Ops II, Call of Duty: Black Ops III, Call of Duty: Black Ops 4, Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War, Call of Duty: Vanguard, Call of Duty: Advanced Warfare, Call of Duty: World War II, and Call of Duty: Infinite Warfare.
